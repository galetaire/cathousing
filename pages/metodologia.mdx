# Methodology üìì

The statistical information presented on this website refers to Catalonia (zone 1 on the map). The only exception is the wage information,
which refers to Spain (zone 2 on the map). The information presented can also be used to get a very general picture of the housing situation
in Southern Europe (zone 3 on the map).

## Sources
